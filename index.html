    D i s c o v e r i e s

    Mantralaya

    Raghavendra Matha is located in Mantralaya, which is in the Raichur district of Karnataka. It is a significant pilgrimage site dedicated to Sri Raghavendra.

    Anjanadri Betta

    Born to Anjana, lord Hanuman was also called Anjaneya and hence the hill is called Anjanadri hills, believed to be his birthplace,located in koppal district.

    Amrutesvara temple

    Amruteshwara is a Shaivite temple with a Shiva linga brought from River Gandaki in Nepal. A beautiful murti (idol) of Sharadha Devi is installed on its right.Located in Chikkamagaluru district.

    Annapoorneshwari Temple

    Known for the revered Annapoorneeshwari Temple, Horanadu is a holy Hindu town located in Malnad, Karnataka.

    Basaveshwara statue

    The 111-foot statue of Basaveshwara, located in Bhishma Lake, Gadag district, Karnataka, is the tallest statue of Basavanna in India, standing in the middle of a 103-acre tank.

    Glass House

    Davanagere Glass House in the botanical garden of the city, Karnataka, India. This is supposed to be the 2nd largest in Asia (to be verified).

    Omkareshwar Temple

    This temple hosts 12 representatives of the original jyotirlingas spread across various states of India. The temple is located in Omkar Ashram,Omkar hills.

    Mandaragiri Hills

    Mandaragiri Hills in Tumkur, Karnataka, India is home to a Jain temple that resembles a bunch of peacock feathers

    Suvarna Vidhana Soudha

    Belgaum hosted the 39th Indian National Congress session in 1924 and was a key British military base due to its proximity to Goa.

    Kotilingeshwara Temple

    The Kotilingeshwara Temple, located in the Kolar district of Karnataka, is renowned for its staggering collection of over 10 million Shiva lingas, making it a prominent spiritual and cultural landmark.

    Male Mahadeshwara Hills

    Male Mahadeshwara Hills in Chamarajanagar district is spiritual and scenic destination with an ancient Lord Mahadeshwara temple and rich wildlife.
